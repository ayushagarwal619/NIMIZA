# NIMIZA 🌟  
### Helping Kids Grow Curious, Aware & Confident
NIMIZA is an interactive learning platform designed to help children build **general awareness, curiosity, confidence, and essential life skills** through storytelling, characters, and playful digital experiences.
The goal of NIMIZA is simple:  
Turn **screen time into meaningful learning time** for children.
Through stories, games, and adventures, NIMIZA helps kids explore the world around them while developing skills that support their growth and understanding.

# 🌍 The Idea
Children today spend a large amount of time on screens.  
However, most digital content focuses mainly on **entertainment instead of meaningful learning**.
NIMIZA aims to change that.
NIMIZA combines:
📖 Storytelling  
🎭 Interactive characters  
🎮 Learning games  
🧠 Awareness-based learning  
to create a **fun and engaging learning environment for kids aged 3–8**.
The platform helps children explore everyday situations, develop curiosity, and learn important life skills while having fun.

# 🎭 Meet the Characters
NIMIZA introduces three characters who guide kids through adventures and learning experiences.

### 🟡 Nino – The Curious Explorer
Nino loves discovering new things.  
He encourages kids to ask questions, explore ideas, and stay curious about the world.

### 🔵 Miko – The Kind Friend
Miko teaches the importance of kindness, cooperation, and understanding others.

### 🟣 Zara – The Brave Problem Solver
Zara inspires children to think independently, solve problems, and believe in themselves.

Each character helps children learn in a **friendly and relatable way**.
