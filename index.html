🌳 Nino and the Magic Tree

A story about teamwork and friendship

One sunny morning, Nino discovered a glowing tree in the park. Miko wondered why it sparkled so brightly in the golden light. Zara said the tree glows when friends help each other. The three friends watered the tree together, shared the watering can without arguing, and suddenly the tree shined brighter than ever. Flowers bloomed all around them as birds came to sing. They learned that teamwork and cooperation make everything magical. ✨
